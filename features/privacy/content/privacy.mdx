    ## Cookies

    I use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

    **What are Cookies?**

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve functionality.

    **How I Use Cookies**

    - Necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g., remembering theme preferences like Light/Dark mode).
    - Used to gather anonymous data on how visitors interact with the site, helping me improve content and performance.

    **Managing Cookies**

    You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You may also use the consent manager provided on this site to adjust your preferences. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
